Smart scheduling

AI, on your terms

Bring your own key. Pick your provider.

Bract supports Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, and Google Gemini. Bring your own API key — pricing is set by the provider you choose. One in-app unlock works with all three. Switch any time without losing your keys.

The only data that leaves your device is event titles and times, only when you use an AI feature, and only to the provider you chose.
