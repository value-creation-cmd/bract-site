Features

What Bract adds to Apple Calendar.

  • Protected blocks

    Mark recurring time as Focus, Sleep, or Family. AI suggestions respect these blocks.

  • Rituals

    Daily and weekly habits that live in your calendar — not in a separate app. Streaks and a 7-day strip make consistency visible.

  • Smart scheduling

    Describe events in plain language. Bract finds slots that respect your protected time.

  • Weekly Review

    See what got done, what slipped, and where your week actually went. AI insights are optional.

  • Widgets

    Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets — rituals, today's events, and morning brief — so your day is visible before you open the app.

  • Private

    API keys stay in your Keychain. Calendar data never leaves your device.
